More Than Soup — We’re Part of Sarnia’s Story

For over 20 years, Soups Up has been a beloved gathering spot in Sarnia, serving comforting meals to families and friends alike. As we face challenges from rising costs and a slow summer, we invite you to join **The Soup Loop** and help us continue our legacy of community support.
